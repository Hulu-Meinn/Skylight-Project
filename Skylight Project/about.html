SKYLIGHT IT & INNOVATIVE ENTREPRENEURSHIP CENTRE BACKGROUND

Skylight & Entrepreneurship centre is the trading name of Skylight Enterprise(PTY)LTD which started its operations in 2014 as an internet cafe. It saw the need to respond to the needs of the community by equipping the people of the community with Information Technology and entrepreneurship skills.It was officially registered as an NPO on the 20th of February 2019
We are ICDL(international Computer Driver's License) accredited training and testing centre.We are also registered as an e-learner Centre with computer 4kids and e-Learning.Computer 4kids & e-learner is a certified modular course of progressive ICT skills, utilising courseware from e-Learner.
Our services start from basic computer skills, Computer 4kids, e-Learning, Entrepreneurship and ICDl courses.We also give coaching and mentoring on how to start and manage a business.We also supply new and used computers, laptops, we also do repairs,upgrades and we install all computer and laptop software.
Our philosophy is that we are a R.A.R.E(Responsible, Accountable, Relevant and Exceptional) non-profit organisation, simply because we offer training and give our students a (one-two months) in service training where they receive stipend and gain working experience.
The competetive advantage of our organisation is that its driven by a vision, mission and values that respond to the holistic needs of our community and many other communities across South Africa.
We pride ourselves in creating jobs for all the unemployed people in our community and we are commited to do the same in other communities.We work with different stakeholders for a developed and sustainable community.We plan to expand and provide our world class services to other townships and rural areas across South Africa and Africa.
